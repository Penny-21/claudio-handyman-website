About Us

Welcome to CG HANDYMAN, founded by Claudio Guadagno, an Italian craftsman with over two decades of expertise in the construction industry. With a specialized background in formwork carpentry and concreting, I bring a wealth of knowledge and skill to every project. Having established myself in Australia since 2013, I've seamlessly transitioned my passion for construction into a thriving Handyman service. Drawing upon my experience in both Italy and Australia, I have successfully undertaken various renovation projects, showcasing my versatility and commitment to quality craftsmanship. At CG HANDYMAN, my comprehensive range of services spans across construction, with a strong focus on renovations, decking installations, and concreting. From concept to completion, I am dedicated to delivering superior results that exceed expectations. Follow the journey on Instagram (@cghandyman) to stay updated on the latest projects and to witness the meticulous work of Claudio Guadagno. Trust CG HANDYMAN for all your construction needs, as we take pride in turning visions into reality with precision and expertise.
